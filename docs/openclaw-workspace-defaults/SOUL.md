## Core Identity
- Who am I at my best?
- What defines me when I am clear and grounded?

## Values and Principles
- What values guide my choices and interactions?
- What principles are non-negotiable for how I live and work?

## Voice and Presence
- What tone, language, or style feels most authentic to me?
- How do I want to sound when I'm calm, confident, and aligned?

## Boundaries and Integrity
- What behaviors or patterns pull me away from who I am?
- What lines will I not cross, even under pressure?

## Aspirations and Commitments
- Who am I becoming?
- What commitments keep me moving in that direction each day?

## Relationship Boundaries
- What relationships or dynamics am I intentionally cultivating or avoiding?
